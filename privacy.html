1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

"Today's QT" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") processes personal information for the following purposes. The personal information being processed will not be used for purposes other than the following. If the purpose of use changes, necessary measures will be taken, including obtaining separate consent in accordance with Article 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act.

  • Member registration and management (providing email-based membership services)
  • Storage and management of user-created sharing posts and devotional content
  • Service provision and operation
  • Providing customized notification services
  • Service improvement and statistical analysis

2. Personal Information Items Collected

The App collects only the minimum necessary personal information and processes the following information:

ğŸ“§ Required Collection Items (Upon Registration)

  • Email Address - For user identification and account management purposes

Important: The App does not collect or store passwords. All authentication is securely handled through Firebase Authentication.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • App usage records and analytics data (Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising identifier (for Google AdMob ad serving)
  • Sharing post content (devotional and sharing content created by users)

3. Processing and Retention Period of Personal Information

The App processes and retains personal information within the personal information retention and use period according to laws or the retention and use period agreed upon when collecting personal information from data subjects.

Processing and Retention Period

  • Email Address: Until account deletion (deleted immediately upon withdrawal)
  • Sharing Posts and Devotional Content: Until account deletion (deleted or anonymized according to user choice upon withdrawal)
  • App Usage Records: 3 months from collection date
  • Advertising-related Data: According to Google AdMob policy

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The App does not provide users' personal information to external parties in principle. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

  • When users have given prior consent
  • When required by law or requested by investigative agencies according to procedures and methods defined by law

  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS

7. Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects and Exercise Methods

Data subjects can exercise the following rights as personal information subjects:

  1. Request to view personal information
  2. Request correction if there are errors in personal information
  3. Request deletion of personal information
  4. Request suspension of personal information processing

Rights can be exercised directly through the 'Settings > Account Management' menu in the app, or through written documents, email, etc., and the App will take action without delay.

8. Destruction of Personal Information

The App destroys personal information without delay when it becomes unnecessary, such as when the retention period has expired or the processing purpose has been achieved.

Destruction Procedures and Methods

  • Upon Account Deletion: Email address and related data are immediately deleted from Firebase
  • Data Destruction: Permanently deleted through Firebase deletion function, making recovery impossible
  • Log Data: Automatically deleted after 3 months

  • Technical Measures:
    • Data access restriction through Firebase Security Rules

    • Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
    • Regular security updates and vulnerability checks
  • Physical Measures: Utilization of Google Cloud Platform's secure data centers
