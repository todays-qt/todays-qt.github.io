1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

"Today's QT" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") processes personal information for the following purposes. The personal information being processed will not be used for purposes other than the following. If the purpose of use changes, necessary measures will be taken, including obtaining separate consent in accordance with applicable laws.

  • Member registration and management (Firebase-based membership services)
  • Storage and management of user-created sharing posts and devotional content
  • Service provision and operation
  • Providing customized notification services
  • Service improvement and statistical analysis

2. Personal Information Items Collected

The App collects only the minimum necessary personal information and processes the following information:

ğŸ” Social Login Information (Managed by Firebase)

  • Email Address - Received from social login providers (Google, Apple, Kakao) and managed by Firebase Authentication
  • Social Login Provider Information - Information about which service (Google/Apple/Kakao) was used for login
  • Profile Information - Basic profile information provided by social login providers (name, etc., optional)

Important: The App does not collect or store passwords. Users log in conveniently with their Google, Apple, or Kakao accounts, and all authentication is securely handled through each social login provider and Firebase Authentication. Email addresses and authentication information are NOT stored on the app server.

ğŸ“ Information Stored on App Server

  • Firebase User ID (UID) - Used only for user identification and data linking
  • Sharing Posts and Devotional Content - Text content created directly by users
  • Creation Date/Time - For content management and display purposes

ğŸ” Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • App usage records and analytics data (Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising identifier (for Google AdMob ad serving)

3. Processing and Retention Period of Personal Information

The App processes and retains personal information within the personal information retention and use period according to laws or the retention and use period agreed upon when collecting personal information from data subjects.

Processing and Retention Period

  • Firebase UID: Until account deletion (deleted immediately from app server upon withdrawal)
  • Sharing Posts and Devotional Content: Until account deletion (deleted or anonymized according to user choice upon withdrawal)
  • Email Address (Firebase): Until account deletion (managed by Firebase)
  • App Usage Records: 3 months from collection date
  • Advertising-related Data: According to Google AdMob policy

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The App does not provide users' personal information to external parties in principle. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

  • When users have given prior consent
  • When required by law or requested by investigative agencies according to procedures and methods defined by law

7. Google AdMob Advertising Service

The App provides advertisements through Google AdMob, and the following information may be processed during this process:

Information Collected

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information and IP address
  • App usage information and ad interaction data

Ad Personalization Settings

Users can opt out of personalized ads in device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (turn off)

For more information about Google's advertising services and privacy practices:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob and Advertising: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

8. Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects

Data subjects can exercise the following rights as personal information subjects:

  1. Request to view personal information
  2. Request correction if there are errors in personal information
  3. Request deletion of personal information
  4. Request suspension of personal information processing

Rights can be exercised directly through the 'Settings > Account Management' menu in the app, or through email, and the App will take action without delay.

9. Destruction of Personal Information

The App destroys personal information without delay when it becomes unnecessary, such as when the retention period has expired or the processing purpose has been achieved.

Destruction Procedures and Methods

  • Upon Account Deletion:
    • Firebase UID and sharing post data are immediately deleted from the app server
    • Email address and social login integration information are deleted from Firebase
    • Google, Apple, and Kakao accounts themselves are managed by each service provider and are not deleted
  • Data Destruction: Permanently deleted, making recovery impossible
  • Log Data: Automatically deleted after 3 months

10. Measures to Ensure Personal Information Security

The App takes the following measures to ensure the security of personal information:

  • Administrative Measures: Minimization and training of personal information handling staff
  • Technical Measures:
    • Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
    • Encryption of all data transmission via HTTPS
    • Server access control and permission management
    • Regular security updates and vulnerability checks
  • Physical Measures: Utilization of secure data centers and access control

11. International Data Transfers

To provide global services, user data may be transferred across borders:

  • Firebase and Google services use data centers worldwide
  • All data transfers are made with appropriate security measures
  • Data protection laws of your country of residence may apply
  • Data is processed through Google's global infrastructure and complies with applicable regional laws
